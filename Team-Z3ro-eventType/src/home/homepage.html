Welcome to your personal Calendar


Palendar is a calendar application. It helps you to keep track of all of your daily raminders and tasks. You may think, "Why should I use this and not any other calendar thats already out their?" Well, let me tell you. Palendar is completely customizable. You can set daily reminders for things like taking medication. You can set reminders for upcoming events like parties or appointments. The best part is you can color code your events. This helps with keeping track of which activity goes into what ever category of your life it fits in.
